NYC Bridges

and how to walk across them

Brooklyn Bridge

The Brooklyn Bridge crosses over the East River connecting Brooklyn and Manhattan. You can walk or bike on the pedestrian walkway above the cars. There is no toll to cross this bridge.

Manhattan Bridge

The Manhattan Bridge crosses over the East River connecting Manhattan and Brooklyn. You can walk or bike on a level below the cars and alongside the B, D, Q, and N trains. There is no toll to cross this bridge.
